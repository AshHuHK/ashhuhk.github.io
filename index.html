About me

Hello, this is Zhihang(Ash) Hu. I received B.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in mathematics and minoring in computer science. I went to the industry, including Baidu and CertusNet between 16-17. Starting from 2017, I initiated my Ph.D. in Computer Science studies at New Jersey Institute of Technology, United States. During four years up to 2021, my research and projects mainly spread across computer vision, generative models, differential privacy and AI for Astronomy. In the meantime, on late 2020, I started working in Bioinformatics and joined the Chinese University of Hong Kong as a Ph.D student, supervised by Prof. Irwin King and Prof. Yu Li, my research then mainly focused on Protein-RNA, Drug related studies. I've now graduated from the states and continue to explore more among the world of AI*Bio.
